Dungeons&Dragons

Tabletop games rather often include chances for randomness by using a thing called dice. One of the most popular Tabletop game Dungeons and dragons use many types of dice:

  • A four sided dice
  • A six sided dice
  • A eight sided dice
  • A ten sided dice
  • A twelve sided dice
  • A twenty sided dice
